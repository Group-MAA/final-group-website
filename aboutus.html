MAA story

Antonella, Majda and Apolline

MAA is the story of 3 young ladies who met all across the world back in 2017. From this meeting, came up an incredible friendship based on a common passion; fashion. A trendy and respectdul brand which will enable women to feel powerful was created. Four years later MAA finally came to life. This brand is the combination of 3 unique personalities and a shared passion… The perfect recipe that we have been sharing with you for months.

Majda,this crazy buble of energy and kindness, is our main designer.

Antonella is in charge of all MAA's communication and team.

Apolline is our favorite tiny human and manage our production and sustainability.
